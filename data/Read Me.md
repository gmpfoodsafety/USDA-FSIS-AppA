This data folder contains all the data fies used to develop this USDA FSIS Appendix A calculator.

Meat 5.0, 6.5,and 7.0 are the time-temperatures for 5, 6.5, and 7.0 log10 reduction of Salmonella in meat.  Meat.csv consolidates the 3 meat files into a single file.

Chicken and Turkey 7.0 are the time-temperatures for 7.0 log10 reduction of Salmonella in chickeen and turkey and contain all the values for fat 1% to 12%.

All.csv consolidates all the .csv data for meat and poultry into a single master time-temperature table.  Note that this file is sparse and may not have time or temps for some products due to the fact that the raw source files do not always have matching times and temperatures.

Only the final All.csv is necessary to produce the calculator .html file with HTML/CSS/Javascript.
